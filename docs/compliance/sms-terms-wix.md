## Text Message Program Terms

These Text Message Program Terms (“SMS Terms”) describe how Otomoto Toronto Moto Inc., doing business as **Toronto Moto** (“we,” “us,” or “our”), sends text messages to customers who opt in. They supplement our general Terms of Service and [Privacy Policy](https://www.torontomoto.com/privacy-policy).

### Program overview

**Program name:** Toronto Moto Text Alerts

**Message types:**

1. **Service updates (transactional / customer care)** — work-order approvals, service status, contracts, payment links, pickup readiness, appointment-related notices, and other messages about your motorcycle service.
2. **Promotional offers (optional marketing)** — occasional promotions, events, seasonal offers, and shop news. Marketing messages are sent only if you separately opt in to promotional texts.

You may opt in to one or both message types. Consent to service updates does not mean you have consented to marketing messages.

### How to opt in

You can enroll by:

- Visiting our public sign-up page: https://service.torontomoto.com/sms
- Providing your mobile number during in-shop intake or customer profile creation and checking the applicable consent box(es)
- Updating preferences in your secure customer portal link
- Other methods where we clearly disclose these SMS Terms at the point of collection

Checkboxes are **not** pre-selected. You must affirmatively choose the program(s) you want.

### Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on your active service, appointments, and (if opted in) promotional campaigns. You may receive several service-related messages during an active work order and fewer messages when you have no open service.

### Costs

**Message and data rates may apply.** Check with your mobile carrier for details about your text and data plan.

### Help and opt-out

- Reply **HELP** (or **INFO** or **SUPPORT**) for help.
- Reply **STOP** (or **STOPALL**, **UNSUBSCRIBE**, **CANCEL**, **END**, **QUIT**, or **HALT**) to cancel all Toronto Moto text messages.
- Reply **START** or **UNSTOP** to re-enable messaging after a STOP; this clears the carrier block but does not by itself restore prior marketing or service consents—you may need to opt in again through our sign-up page or portal.

After you opt out, we will send a one-time confirmation and stop further messages unless you opt in again.

### Support

For help with our text program or your service account, visit **https://www.torontomoto.com**. We do not provide SMS support by reply beyond the automated HELP response.

### Carrier liability

Wireless carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Delivery depends on your carrier, device, and network conditions.

### Privacy

Our [Privacy Policy](https://www.torontomoto.com/privacy-policy) explains how we handle personal information, including SMS opt-in data. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are not shared with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

### Eligibility

You must be the subscriber or authorized user of the mobile number you provide and be able to receive text messages in Canada or the United States, as applicable. You agree to provide an accurate mobile number.

### Changes

We may modify or discontinue the text program or these SMS Terms. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
