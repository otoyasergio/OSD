## Privacy Policy

Otomoto Toronto Moto Inc. (“Toronto Moto,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) operates https://www.torontomoto.com and provides motorcycle service, repair, and related customer communications. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information when you visit our website, book service, use our customer portal, or interact with us by phone, email, or text message.

### Who we are

- **Legal name:** Otomoto Toronto Moto Inc.
- **Doing business as:** Toronto Moto
- **Contact:** https://www.torontomoto.com

### Information we collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information:

1. **Contact and identity information** — name, phone number, email address, mailing address, and similar details you provide when booking service, creating a customer profile, or contacting us.
2. **Service and vehicle information** — motorcycle make, model, year, VIN or plate details, service history, work-order notes, and related shop records.
3. **Booking and appointment information** — appointment dates, service requests, and intake details from our website, Wix Bookings, or in-shop intake.
4. **Payment and billing information** — invoice details, payment status, and transaction references. Payment card data is processed by our payment providers; we do not store full card numbers on our servers.
5. **Communications** — messages you send us by email, phone, or SMS, including replies such as approvals, questions, or opt-out requests.
6. **Website and technical information** — IP address, browser type, device information, cookies, and usage data collected through our website and analytics tools.
7. **Customer portal activity** — actions taken when you access a secure portal link we send you (for example, reviewing estimates, signing documents, or updating preferences).

### How we use information

We use personal information to:

- Provide motorcycle service, diagnostics, repair, and pickup coordination
- Communicate about appointments, work orders, approvals, contracts, payments, and pickup readiness
- Send optional promotional offers when you have separately opted in to marketing messages
- Operate our website, customer portal, and internal workshop systems
- Process payments and maintain billing records
- Improve our services, security, and customer experience
- Comply with legal, tax, and regulatory obligations

### How we share information

We may share personal information with service providers that help us operate our business, such as:

- Website and booking platforms (including Wix)
- Payment processors (including Square)
- Email and SMS delivery providers (including Twilio)
- Cloud hosting and database providers
- Professional advisors where required by law

We require these providers to use information only to perform services for us and to protect it appropriately.

We may also disclose information when required by law, to protect our rights or safety, or in connection with a business transaction such as a merger or asset sale.

**SMS opt-in exclusion (required clause):**

All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information won’t be shared with any third parties.

### Text messaging

If you opt in to Toronto Moto text alerts, we collect and store your mobile number, consent choices (service updates and/or promotional offers), consent timestamps, and related opt-in/opt-out records. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply **HELP** for help or **STOP** to cancel. For program details, see our [Terms of Service / SMS Program Terms](https://www.torontomoto.com/terms).

You may sign up for text alerts at https://service.torontomoto.com/sms, through staff during intake, through your customer portal, or by other methods we describe at the point of collection. We do not sell your phone number or SMS consent data.

### Cookies and analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, measure traffic, and improve the site. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Some site features may not work correctly if cookies are disabled.

### Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed to provide services, meet legal and accounting requirements, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. SMS consent and opt-out records are retained to demonstrate compliance with messaging laws and carrier requirements.

### Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

### Your choices

Depending on applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of certain personal information, or object to some processing. To opt out of marketing emails or promotional SMS, use the unsubscribe method in the message or contact us through our website. Reply **STOP** to opt out of text messages.

### Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

### Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above will change when we do. Continued use of our services after an update means you accept the revised policy.
